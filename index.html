Yoctor

Have you ever feel annoyed by wasted time while waiting for a doctor’s appointment? We live in a hasty world and can’t afford to lose time with waiting rooms.  Yoctor is here to solve your problems. What if you could know the actual time of your appointment with a doctor? We can give you precise previsions to appointment hours, to reduce you idleness in waiting rooms, valuing you and your time. Our solution for you: Develop integrated apps for you, the patient, for clinics and doctors, managing the schedule, mapping the appointments in real time, allowing us, to give you precise predictions to solve your problem.
For more informations about technologies (APIs, architecture and more) about the app, just click the github logo on the picture.

Bacon Runner

Bacon Runner is a iOS 2D Endless Runner game with ranking between friends, generating higher competition, using a fun theme.
The game tells a story about a little pig that wants to survive human attacks and not become bacon, the world's most desired food and, at the same time, extremely rare in the story context.
Being an Endless Runner, your goal is to avoid all the attacks and obstacles that shows up and to collect as much apples as possible, as it is the pig's most valuable fuel. Developed with João Pereira e Gustavo Olenka. Drew by Rafael Araújo.
For more informations about technologies (APIs, architecture and more) about the app, just click the github logo on the picture.

Run, Pig, Run!

Run, Pig, Run! is a iOS 2D Endless Runner game with ranking between friends, generating higher competitivity, using a fun theme.
The game tells a story about a little pig that wants to survive human attacks and not become bacon, the world's most desired food and, at the same time, extremely rare in the story context.
Being an Endless Runner, your goal is to avoid all the attacks and obstacles that shows up and to collect as much coins and power ups as possible to become stronger and run farther.
Developed with João Pereira e Gustavo Olenka.

DRUL

Dream Teller

Dream Teller is a Swift Playground application made for thw WWDC 2017 Scholarship submission. This project was selected and I won free tickets to the WWDC. The project uses multiple interactions with the user while is telling a story about a kid. The goal of this app is to motivate and teach kids how to code and what the can do coding.
For more informations about technologies (APIs, architecture and more) about the app, just click the github logo on the picture.

CrowdSearch

CrowdSearch is my newest creation. It's an app developed to help people searching on the internet. This apps makes possible to search anything anywhere in the world with no VPN. With the Bing Web Search API, I developed this application thats connects users all over the world. You just need to tap what you want to search, and where. Someone will make the search for you with just one tap on the screen.
For more informations about technologies (APIs, architecture and more) about the app, just click the github logo on the picture.

Social Wallet

Social Wallet is an app that helps you to split a bill in a bar or restaurant. You can also track how much you owe to a friend and your frieds to you. It's a financial controller for all the bills you want to split.
For more informations about technologies (APIs, architecture and more) about the app, just click the github logo on the picture.
